Started by WWF and partners as a symbolic br lights-out
event in Sydney in 2007, Earth Hour is now one of the world's
largest grassroots movements for the environment. Held
every year on the last Saturday of March, Earth Hour engages
millions of people in more than 180 countries and territories,
switching off their lights to show support for our planet.
Earth hour is about spreading awareness of environmental
issues. It is an annual global event organized by WWF
(World Wild Fund for Nature) encouraging individuals,
communities.
